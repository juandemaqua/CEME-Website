Our Audition Process 我们的面试流程

Prepare your repertoire

For all Eastern & Western instruments, prepare the following:

  • A self-selected piece | 5 min
  • FOR PIANO. select 2 contrasting pieces (both within 15min)
  • Scales (major, minor scales, arpeggios)
  • Sight-reading (with given time for preparation during the audition)
  • A brief self-introduction

Apply using the form linked below on our Instagram. Practice, practice, practice! The audition will take place in early February at Lincoln Hall. More information will be sent after you apply.
